# Privacy Policy for Calculator App

**Last Updated:** January 16, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Calculator App ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

## Summary

**We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.** The Calculator App is designed to operate entirely offline and locally on your device.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

The Calculator App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Device identifiers or unique IDs
- Location data
- Usage analytics or statistics
- Calculation history beyond your current session
- Cookies or tracking technologies
- Any form of user-generated content

## How the App Works

- **Local Processing Only:** All calculations are performed entirely on your device using local processing. No data is sent to external servers.
- **No Internet Required:** The App does not require or use internet connectivity.
- **No Account Required:** The App does not require user registration or login.
- **Session-Based History:** Calculation history is stored only in temporary memory during your current session and is automatically cleared when you close the App or press the clear button.

## Data Storage

- **No Persistent Storage:** The App does not save any data to your device's permanent storage.
- **No Cloud Storage:** The App does not use cloud services or remote databases.
- **No Backups:** The App has disabled Android's backup feature (`android:allowBackup="false"`), meaning no app data is backed up to Google's servers or any other backup service.

## Third-Party Services

The Calculator App does not integrate with or use any third-party services, including:

- Analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
- Advertising networks
- Social media platforms
- Cloud services
- Crash reporting tools

## Children's Privacy

The Calculator App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. The App is safe for use by users of all ages.

## Permissions

The Calculator App requests **no special permissions** on your device. It does not access:

- Camera or microphone
- Contacts or call logs
- Storage or files
- Location services
- Network or internet

## Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any user data, there is no risk of data breach or unauthorized access to your personal information through our App.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over your device and can uninstall the App at any time.
